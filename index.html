✦ The Sacred Meaning ✦

The Spirit of
Eid Al-Adha

"Indeed, We have granted you Al-Kawthar. So pray to your Lord and sacrifice. Indeed, your enemy is the one cut off."
— Surah Al-Kawthar (108:1–3)

Eid Al-Adha — Qurbani ka Tyohaar — Hazrat Ibrahim ﷺ ki gehri aqeedat ki yaad dilaata hai, jinhon ne Allah ki marzi ke aage mukammal taur par sar jhuka diya. Yeh iman, shukr, aur reham dili aur baatne ki timeless values ka celebration hai.

Qurbani ka amal sirf ek rasm nahi — yeh ek yaad dahaani hai ke sachchi aqeedat ke liye qurbani zaroori hai, ke sakhawat insaan ki rooh ko badal deti hai, aur ke Allah se hamara rishta un lamhon mein tajdeed hota hai jab hum poori tarah surrender kar dete hain.
